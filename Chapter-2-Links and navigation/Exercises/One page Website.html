Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an epic fantasy television series created by David Benioff and D.B. Weiss, based on the series of novels "A Song of Ice and Fire" by George R.R. Martin. The show premiered on HBO on April 17, 2011, and concluded on May 19, 2019, spanning eight seasons. Set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, the story features numerous characters and intricate plots centered around power struggles, political intrigue, and territorial wars among noble families.

Cast & Creative Team

  • Emilia Clarke as Daenerys Targaryen
  • Kit Harington as Jon Snow
  • Peter Dinklage as Tyrion Lannister
  • Lena Headey as Cersei Lannister
  • Maisie Williams as Arya Stark
  • Creative Team: David Benioff, D.B. Weiss (Creators)

Key Characters

Game of Thrones is known for its rich ensemble cast, including major characters like:

  • Daenerys Targaryen: The last surviving member of the Targaryen dynasty.
  • Jon Snow: The honorable member of the Night's Watch.
  • Tyrion Lannister: The clever and witty dwarf of House Lannister.
  • Cersei Lannister: The ruthless queen determined to protect her family.
  • Arya Stark: A fierce and independent young warrior.
