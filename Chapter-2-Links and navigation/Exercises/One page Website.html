Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an epic fantasy television series created by David Benioff and D.B. Weiss, based on the series of novels "A Song of Ice and Fire" by George R.R. Martin. The show premiered on HBO on April 17, 2011, and concluded on May 19, 2019, spanning eight seasons. Set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, the story features numerous characters and intricate plots centered around power struggles, political intrigue, and territorial wars among noble families.
