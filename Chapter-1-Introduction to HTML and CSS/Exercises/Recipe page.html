Mini Pita Bites with Chicken Filling

Ingredients

For Chicken Marination:

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp crushed black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon

For Sriracha-Mayo Sauce:

  • 1 cup (250ml) mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 4 tbsp ketchup

For Pita Dough:

  • 250ml + 130ml warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 7g yeast
  • 4 cups (500g) flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp oil

For Salad:

  • Chopped red onion
  • Chopped cucumber

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

In a bowl, mix the chicken with ginger-garlic paste, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken

Heat a pan over medium heat, add a little oil, and cook the marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Sriracha-Mayo Sauce

In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, Sriracha sauce, and ketchup. Stir well until smooth.

Step 4: Make the Pita Dough

In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.

In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and oil. Knead until a soft dough forms.

Cover and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Step 5: Cook the Pita Bread

Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into mini circles. Cook on a preheated pan until they puff up and are golden brown.

Step 6: Assemble the Mini Pita Bites

Cut the pita in half to create pockets. Fill each pocket with cooked chicken, chopped red onion, and cucumber.

Drizzle with Sriracha-mayo sauce.

Step 7: Serve & Enjoy

Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Mini Pita Bites with Chicken Filling!

Enjoy!
