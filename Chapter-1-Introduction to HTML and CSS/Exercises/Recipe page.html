INGREDIENTS

For Chicken Marination:

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp crushed black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lemon

For Pita Dough:

  • 250ml + 130ml warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 7g yeast
  • 4 cups (500g) flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp oil

For Sriracha-Mayo Sauce:

  • 1 cup (250ml) mayonnaise
  • 3 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 4 tbsp ketchup

For Salad:

  • Chopped red onion
  • Chopped cucumber
